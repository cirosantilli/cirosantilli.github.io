Richard Feynman Quantum Electrodynamics Lecture at University of Auckland (1979) mentions it several times.
🔗
This was perhaps one of the first great successes of QED.
🔗
In https://youtu.be/UKbp85zpdcY?t=52 Freeman Dyson mentions that the original key experiment was from Kusch and Foley from Columbia University, and that in 1948, Julian Schwinger reached the correct value from his calculations.
