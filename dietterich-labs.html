Covers some specific hardcore subjects, notably quantum electrodynamics, in full mathematical detail. Dietterich is a condensed matter PhD candidate or post-doc as of 2020 apparently, living in Minnesota*.
🔗
Unfortunately the channel is too obsessed with mathematical detail (which it does amazingly), and does not give enough examples/application/intuition, which is what would be useful to most people.
🔗
Generally involves short talks + a detailed read-through of a pre-prepared PDF. Dietterich has refused giving the PDF or LaTeX source as of 2020 on comments unfortunately... what a wasted opportunity for society.
🔗
