As shown in Video 135. ""Simple Groups - Abstract Algebra" by Socratica (2018)", this can be split up into two steps:
  • classification of simple finite groups: done
  • group extension problem
This split is sometimes called the "Jordan-Hölder program" in reference to the authors of the jordan-Holder Theorem.
