Magnetic quantum number (m_l)

| 🗖 nosplit | ↑ parent "Atomic orbital" | words: 52
🗖 nosplit | ⇓ toc | ↑ parent "Atomic orbital" | Wikipedia | words: 52
🔗
Fixed angular momentum in a given direction. Note that this direction is arbitrary, since for a fixed azimuthal quantum number (and therefore fixed total angular momentum), we can only know one direction for sure. z is normally used by convention.
