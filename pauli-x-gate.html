Pauli-X gate (Quantum NOT gate)

 ... Quantum computer type Model of quantum computing Analog and digital quantum computers Digital quantum computer Quantum logic gate Single-qubit gate
OurBigBook.com words: 73
The quantum NOT gate swaps the state of ∣0⟩ and ∣1⟩, i.e. it maps:
x∣0⟩+y∣y⟩→y∣0⟩+x∣y⟩
(9)
As a result, this gate also inverts the probability of measuring 0 or 1, e.g.
  • if the old probability of 0 was 0, then it becomes 1
  • if the old probability of 0 was 0.2, then it becomes 0.8
