Tokyo (东京)

 ... List of continents Asia East Asia Country in East Asia Japan City in Japan
OurBigBook.com Words: 71
Literally: "capital of the East". Note that the Chinese term 东京 (dong1 jing1) is highly ambiguous in historical contexts, as many places throughout history have been known as "the capital of the East".
  • en.wikipedia.org/wiki/東京
  • zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/東京_(消歧義)
Of of those other places is the Kaifeng, which is the Capital in Water Margin and in real life during the Song dynasty. The novel is also likely highly popular in Japan apparently as well BTW, e.g. it merited a well funded adaptation: The Water Margin.
