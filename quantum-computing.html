Quantum is getting hot in 2019, and even Ciro Santilli got a bit excited: quantum computing could be the next big thing.
🔗
No useful algorithm has been economically accelerated by quantum yet, but the bets are on.
🔗
To get a feeling of this, just have a look at the insane number of startups that are already developing quantum algorithms for hardware that doesn't/barely exists! https://quantumcomputingreport.com/players/privatestartup (archive).
