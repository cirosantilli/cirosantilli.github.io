System of partial differential equations | ↑ parent "Differential equation" | nosplit

🔗
In many important applications, what you have to solve is not just a single partial differential equation, but multiple partial differential equations coupled to each other. This is the case for many key PDEs including:
  • Maxwell's equations, see: Section 2.2. "Explicit scalar form of the Maxwell's equations"
  • Navier-Stokes equations
  • Schrodinger equation, see: Section 5.4.1.2.1. "Why are complex numbers used in the Schrodinger equation?"
