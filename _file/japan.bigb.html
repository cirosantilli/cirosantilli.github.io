= 东京
{synonym}
{title2}

Literally: "capital of the East". Note that the <Chinese (language)> term 东京 (dong1 jing1) is highly ambiguous in historical contexts, as many places throughout history have been known as "the capital of the East".
* https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/東京
* https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/東京_(消歧義)
Of of those other places is the <Kaifeng>, which is <the Capital (Water Margin location)> in <Water Margin> and in real life during the <Song dynasty>. The novel is also likely highly popular in Japan apparently as well BTW, e.g. it merited a well funded adaptation: <The Water Margin (1973 TV series)>.
