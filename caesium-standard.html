Caesium standard (1967)

| 🗖 nosplit | ↑ parent "Atomic clock" | words: 85
🗖 nosplit | ⇓ toc | ↑ parent "Atomic clock" | Wikipedia | words: 85
🔗
Uses the frequency of the hyperfine structure of caesium-133 ground state (6s1) to define the second.
🔗
International System of Units definition os the second since 1967, because this is what atomic clocks use.
🔗
Why caesium hyperfine structure is used:
  • https://physics.stackexchange.com/questions/191871/why-do-atomic-clocks-only-use-caesium
