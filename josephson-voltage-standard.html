Josephson voltage standard (JVS)

 ... Physics Condensed matter physics Electrical resistivity and conductivity Superconductivity Josephson effect Applications of Josephson Junctions

The most practical/precise volt standard.
It motivated the definition of the ampere in the 2019 redefinition of the SI base units
