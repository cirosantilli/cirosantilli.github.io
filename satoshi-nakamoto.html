Domain registration: 2008-08-18 by https://www.namecheap.com, an United States company. But using a privacy oriented registrar: https://bitcoin.stackexchange.com/questions/89532/how-did-nakamoto-untraceably-pay-for-registering-bitcoin-org

2008-08-22: private Wei Dai email: https://www.gwern.net/docs/bitcoin/2008-nakamoto from gwern.net
🔗
Paper announcement: 2008-10-31 https://www.metzdowd.com/pipermail/cryptography/2008-October/014810.html linking to bitcoin.org, from satoshi@vistomail.com Claimed one year and a half development time.
🔗
Replies in November: https://www.metzdowd.com/pipermail/cryptography/2008-November/thread.html#14863 under satoshi@anonymousspeech.com claims source code shared privately by request at that point.
🔗
It is unknown how he could have paid anonymously: https://bitcoin.stackexchange.com/questions/89532/how-did-nakamoto-untraceably-pay-for-registering-bitcoin-org, so it seems likely that the true identity could be obtained by law enforcement if needed.
🔗
